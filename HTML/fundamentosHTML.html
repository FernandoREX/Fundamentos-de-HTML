Day of the Dead

Origin of Tradition

Death comes to all of us

The celebration of the deceased faithful in Mexico has its origin in pre-Hispanic times.
According to historians, the Mexica had various periods throughout the year to celebrate their dead , the most important of which took place at the end of the harvest, between the months of September and November.
